FAQ

Will food be provided at the reception?

Yes, a full dinner will be served at the reception. Please note that dietary restrictions can be accommodated with advance notice.

Can we bring a gift to give to the couple on their special day?

While gifts are not required or expected, they are always appreciated.

Is photography and/or videography allowed during the ceremony and reception?

Yes, official photographers and videographers will be capturing the wedding. We also encourage you to take your own photos and videos on the day. However, please refrain from taking photos or videos during the ceremony itself.

Are children allowed at the ceremony and/or reception?

We're having an adult-only celebration at our wedding, which means no kids will be allowed. We hope you can understand and respect this decision so we can create a wonderful experience for everyone involved.
